Description

Subject-2003 (designated "The Alamban") is a 22-year-old humanoid specimen exhibiting minimal muscle mass and irregular nutritional patterns. Preliminary analysis suggests mild malnutrition due to caloric prioritization of snacks over meals. The subject is currently an IT student demonstrating high aptitude for digital manipulation. Observable traits include black hair and a consistent preference for dark-colored attire to minimize visual detection.

Subject-2003 was first documented at a 7/11 convenience store following reports of an agitated individual exhibiting hunger-driven bargaining behavior. Upon contact, the subject was observed biting a sandwich while demanding a price reduction due to perceived overvaluation.

Special Containment Procedures

Subject-2003 is to be maintained in a standard residential chamber located in Poblacion, Opol. A steady supply of sandwiches and high-speed internet access is required to maintain docility.

During “Hunger Events,” the subject may migrate toward the nearest 7/11 checkpoint. Personnel are advised not to disrupt the "Procrastination Cycle" unless a deadline is within two hours, at which point the Subject enters a hyper-productive frenzy state.

Behavioral Patterns

Subject engages in 4–6 hours of daily “Digital Foraging,” navigating online information networks. Notable proficiencies include Video Games (Primary) and Coding (Secondary).
